Who we are

The Pet's paw is a charity project whose main goal is to help shelters find new owners for their pets. Our main task is to find a home for as many animals living in shelters as possible.

It is very important for us to make people aware that shelter animals are safe and absolutely normal! We believe that with our joint efforts we will be able to move the situation from a standstill.

What we do

Pet care

We provide lots of health and behavior information to help pet parents take the best care

Pet licensing

You will be named as a legal guardian and every check-ups will be much easier

Neutering

Spaying and neutering help protect against some serious health problems

Finding home

Lots of shelters around the country so you can adopt a pet from anywhere if you wish

How to adopt

Step 1

The first step is the most important and responsible. If you dream of a little friend, first you should decide who you want – a cat or a dog.

Step 2

So, you already know who you want to adopt and give a cozy home. Then you need to fill out the application on the website. Choose the pet type, age, gender, location and leave us your contact details.

Step 3

After your application is processed, we will contact you. We will provide full information on the adoption of the desired animal and invite you to the first meeting to visit the pet and the shelter.
